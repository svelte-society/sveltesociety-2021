Learn 2 Earn


One of the best ways to learn might be to practice providing education services for others.

Here you can earn money while providing education services around topics like decentralization, self-hosted wallets, deflationary currencies like CULT and RVLT, tokenomics, incentive engineering, privacy engineering and with that freedom engineering.




Education Videos


You might consider to create education videos, upload them on rumble.com, add your public wallet address in the video description and register your public wallet address for the CULT Donation Microservice (under construction)




24 / 7 DeFi Consultation Hour


The goal of this 24/7 conference is to mutually provide education and coaching around decentralization, self-hosted wallets etc.
Details on how this could look like are in the cooking. Proposals are of course always welcome.
