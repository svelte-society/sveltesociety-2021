CULTDAO


The CULTDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization which is already CULT.

You can regard a decentralized autonomous organization as a better version of a shareholder company.

The reason why it is a better version of a shareholder company is that it is not ruled by corrupt politicians but by the shareholders.

The goal of the CULTDAO is to support those who support decentralization.

Decentralization is one of the greatest ways to avoid corruption. If everyone has roughly the same rights within a system, there is no ground for corruption.

With CULTDAO we collectively prepare the ground for fair, transparent, fruitful collaboration.

To become a CULTDAO shareholder you need to acquire CULT and stake it.

In order to acquire CULT we recommend to:

1. install metamask.io or another browserwallet you fully trust

2. visit uniswap.org to buy CULT - e.g. paying with Ether

Once you have CULT in your browserwallet, you can stake CULT by:

1. visiting https://app.cultdao.io/stake

2. following the obvious steps

If you face any difficulties feel free to reach out to our discord community. After following the mentioned steps successfully, you are a shareholder of the CULTDAO. That's at least how most of us felt after understanding the beauty of the CULTDAO.
