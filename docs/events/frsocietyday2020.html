Svelte Society Day 2020 France

The Svelte Society Day 2020 was an event for french Svelte developers with eleven french talks on our youtube channel about Svelte related topics like:

  • React-/Angularmigration to Svelte
  • Typescript at Svelte
  • GraphQL
  • routify

To get more information visit the Svelte Society france website.
