Svelte Summit Spring 2021

Svelte Summit is an event dedicated to Svelte and everything that is happening in the community. The event was a day of (exclusive) pre-recorded talks streamed online, syndicated on various platforms including YouTube. Specific discussions about the talks and water-cooler chit-chat happened live in the Svelte Discord server.

Everyone was welcome, to attend and submit a talk at sessionize. Svelte Summit reflected the wider Svelte community as an inclusive and supportive space for people to learn and make friends.
